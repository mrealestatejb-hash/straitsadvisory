About this property

MBW Bay is a landmark freehold seafront township by MB World Group spanning 17 acres at Danga Bay, designed around biophilic architecture principles where nature, sustainability, and wellness are intertwined. The mixed-use development will deliver 2,930 serviced apartments and eight townhouses across three phases with an estimated gross development value of RM2.4 billion. Located just 8km from the JB-Singapore RTS Link and CIQ, the GreenRE-certified development offers up to 30 premium facilities with partially furnished units designed to maximise natural light and connection with the outdoors. With completion spanning 2026-2029, MBW Bay represents a new chapter in sustainable seafront living along Johor Bahru's Danga Bay corridor.
