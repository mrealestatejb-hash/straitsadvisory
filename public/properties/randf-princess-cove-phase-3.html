About R&F Princess Cove Phase 3

R&F Princess Cove Phase 3 — New Casa Suites is the latest phase of the iconic 116-acre integrated waterfront development by R&F Properties. Located in Tanjung Puteri, Johor Bahru, it sits just 650 metres from the Sultan Iskandar CIQ checkpoint and the upcoming RTS Link station, offering seamless 5-minute connectivity to Singapore's Woodlands North.

New Casa Suites offers units ranging from 313 to 1,555 sqft across studio, 1, 2, 3, and 4-bedroom configurations. The development features the Urban Skypark — a rooftop leisure deck with Olympic pool, gym, tennis courts, jogging track, and children's play areas. All residents enjoy access to the existing R&F Mall, cinema, international school, and medical centre within the integrated township.

With freehold tenure, competitive entry prices, and the proven rental demand of the R&F Princess Cove brand, Phase 3 represents a compelling opportunity for Singapore investors seeking capital appreciation and rental income from JB's most connected waterfront address.
