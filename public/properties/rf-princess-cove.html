About this property

R&F Princess Cove is a 116-acre integrated waterfront development by R&F Properties, located in Bukit Chagar, Johor Bahru. Strategically positioned just 1km from the CIQ checkpoint and the upcoming RTS Link station, it is one of the largest mixed-use developments in the Johor Bahru city centre. The masterplan encompasses residential towers across multiple phases, a retail mall, serviced apartments, and commercial spaces — creating a self-contained waterfront city. With freehold tenure, direct strait views, and prices starting from RM350K, R&F Princess Cove represents exceptional value for Singapore buyers seeking premium JB real estate with unrivalled connectivity.
