About this property

R&F Princess Cove Ph2 is a completed freehold executive condominium developed by R&F Properties in Bukit Chagar, Johor. Strategically positioned just 1km from the CIQ checkpoint and the upcoming RTS Link station, it offers unparalleled connectivity to Singapore.

The development features over 70 facilities including multiple swimming pools, a fully-equipped gymnasium, landscaped gardens, children's playgrounds, BBQ areas, and 24-hour security. Units range from 800 to 1,400 sqft with 2 to 3 bedrooms, catering to both owner-occupiers and investors.

With freehold tenure and a starting price from RM5xxk (approximately S$149K SGD), R&F Princess Cove Ph2 represents exceptional value for Singapore buyers looking to invest in the Johor Bahru property market. The development is completed and ready for immediate occupation, eliminating construction risk.

R&F Properties is an established developer with a proven track record of delivering quality residential projects. Their commitment to innovative design and premium finishes is evident throughout Princess Cove Ph2.
